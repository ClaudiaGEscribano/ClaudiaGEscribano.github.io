Claudia Gutiérrez

claudia.g.escribano@gmail.com

PostDoctoral researcher at UCLM. Physicist working on the links between climate and renewable energies. Most of the time I work in wind and photovoltaic energy and its variability. Trying to understand the impact of climate change on the resources.
